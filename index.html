How It Works

01 - Consultation

Share your idea, size, and placement. We’ll guide you through the process.

02 - Custom Design

Your artist creates a unique design tailored specifically to you. We refine the details together so the final concept reflects your style and fits your body perfectly.

03 - Booking

Once the design is approved, we secure your appointment date and review preparation guidelines. A deposit confirms your session and locks in your spot.

04 - Tattoo Session

On the day of your appointment, we finalize placement and sizing before starting. Our focus is precision, comfort, and delivering work that lasts.

05 - Aftercare

We provide clear aftercare instructions to ensure proper healing and long-term quality. Following these steps helps your tattoo stay sharp and vibrant.
